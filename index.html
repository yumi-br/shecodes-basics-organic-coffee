About OCCA

We are a non-profit community that
Supports Women Organic Coffee Farmers
by guaranteeing sales worldwide

Organic Coffee are typically cultivated based on sustainable farming processes, prioritizing the health of the soil. As a natural consequence, it promotes healthness for plants, farmers, consumers and the local biodiversity.

Brazil is by far the world's main coffee producer. However, only 0.3% of the entire production is organic, and there relies an extraordinary opportunity for Brazilian Women Farmers to thrive.

The mission of the Organic Coffee Community Abroad is to empower women who farm organically in Brazil by providing guaranteed production sale,as well asoffering on-demand coursesto enhance their personal skills and businesses.
